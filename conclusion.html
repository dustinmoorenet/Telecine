Conclusion
        I greatly enjoyed designing and building the system. It was a big learning experience, especially in the area of image manipulation. I even built my own class to build, edit, open and save bitmap files, before I learned that the code to manipulate image files has already been throughly written. Even though the system is not reliable, I do not consider the project a failure. In fact, I consider it one of the greatest projects I have ever been apart of.
